Instructions for Squeexle

Every day you'll see a new Squeex!
You have 6 chances to guess the exact date this screenshot is from.

If your guess is correct: the number will be green.
If your guess is close: the number will be yellow.
If your guess is far away: the number will be red.
If your guess is VERY far away: the number will be grey.

For example...
Month is close, day is very far away, year is correct
  • 10
  • 2
  • 2024
Month is correct, day is far away, year is close
  • 7
  • 4
  • 2023

After you get the date correct, you can guess which game or category this screenshot was in.
There is no limit on how many times you can guess this.

Have fun!

Thanks to Soaral for help with adding screenshots!
Inspired by Forsle and Costcodle.

Any issues? Please contact driedapps on Discord.
Keep in mind that dates were mostly taken from Youtube VODs. Actual stream date might be different.
