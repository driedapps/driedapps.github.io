SQUEEXLE

Instructions for Squeexle!

Every day you'll see a new Squeex! Guess which day the stream is from and you'll win. If your guess is close the day, month, or year will be yellow. If your guess is far away the day, month, or year will be red. If your guess is VERY far away the day, month, or year will be black. You can also guess which game Squeex was playing in the stream. Have fun! Any issues? Please contact driedapps on Discord.
